Saif Partners

SAIF Partners is a leading private equity firm that provides growth capital to companies in Asia. The primary areas of focus include Information Technology, Internet, Mobile, Consumer Products & Services, Healthcare, Cleantech, Education, Modern Agriculture, Financial Services and Manufacturing.SAIF Partners was founded in 2001 and currently manages over $3.5 billion in capital. With over 100 investments since its inception, SAIF has become one of the largest and most active funds in the region. SAIF is a long-term investor with local teams in Hong Kong, China, and India..
